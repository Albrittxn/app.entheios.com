# Set Up

Three quick steps. About 5 minutes total.

**Step 1 — Bookmark this tab.**
This is your home base from now on. Your assigned calls show up in the **Calls** tab — upcoming and past, every call sorted by time.

**Step 2 — Join the Cal team.**
[Click here to join the Entheios AI Cal team](https://app.cal.com/teams?token=cc807025cfe7db78167cd80faa69b0f63fd56ad4f486bdb75ce0aa1ce87bfded). When Cal asks you to sign up or sign in, **use your `@entheios.com` email — not a personal Gmail address**. That's what puts you in the rotation AND makes sure your booked calls show up in this portal. Next time a prospect books a discovery call, the system might assign it to you.

**Step 3 — Sign your payment contract.**
Open the **Payment Contract** card just below — it's the independent contractor payout agreement, pre-filled with your commission terms. Review it, sign, and download your copy. Once it's signed the card moves to the bottom of this page and shows as completed. Do this before your first call.

Once all three are done, read **Systems** and **Script** end-to-end and bookmark [slides.entheios.com](http://slides.entheios.com). When your first booking lands, follow **Systems**.
